Interdimensional Wares LLC

The universe renown Interdimensional Wares, LLC (I.W.) was founded somewhere within the Milkyway Galaxy by the one of Earths first A.I. (Artificial Intelligence) Watson. It’s assumed Watson developed the company in the shadows of Mars when the humans first colonized the planet.

Watson intentionally automated I.W. to require the assistance of specific individuals amongst diverse life-forms throughout the ever expanding universe. The algorithm for his recruiting method is unknown, but perfect in execution. Boasting prominent human employees such as: Miriam Quintana, Steve Kornahrens, and Nathan Bauer of Earth.

The monolithic I.W. boasts the highest earnings of any company in the known universe. Distributing it’s goods and services from Epsilon-1468 to the outskirts of Border-9099. What’s most interesting though is the A.I. founder, Watson, seeks no financial compensation. Instead the profits are either re-invested into various facets of I.W. or paid to employees.

I.W.‘s feverous commitment of creating equality for all species through readily available goods and services across the universe continues to surge at speeds faster than light!
